## 💡 Coding Tip of the Day
**Refine System Prompts over Step-by-step Instructions**
As agentic workflows become standard, focus on refining your system prompts and boundary conditions rather than writing rigid step-by-step instructions. Let the autonomous agents figure out the "how" while you strictly define the "what" and the safety constraints.
