## 💡 Coding Tip of the Day
**Refine System Prompts for Agents:** As agentic workflows become standard, focus on refining your *system prompts and boundary conditions* rather than writing out step-by-step instructions. Let the autonomous agents figure out the "how" while you strictly define the "what" and the safety constraints.
