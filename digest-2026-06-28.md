## 💡 Daily Coding Tip
**Refining Agentic Prompts:** As autonomous agentic workflows become the new standard, shift your focus to refining your *system prompts and boundary conditions* rather than writing strict, step-by-step instructions. Let the AI agents figure out the "how" while you strictly define the "what" and the safety constraints.
