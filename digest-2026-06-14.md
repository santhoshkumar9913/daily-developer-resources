## 💡 Coding Tip of the Day
**Shift to Agentic Prompting**
As agentic workflows become standard, focus on refining your *system prompts and boundary conditions* rather than writing step-by-step instructions. Let the agents figure out the "how" while you strictly define the "what," the expected outputs, and the safety constraints.
