## 💡 Coding Tip of the Day
**Optimize for Prompt Caching:**
When working with LLM APIs that support prompt caching (like Anthropic or OpenAI), always structure your prompts so the static/reusable parts (such as system instructions, schemas, and large context documents) are placed at the *beginning*, followed by the dynamic parts (user queries) at the end. This maximizes cache hits, drastically reducing latency and preventing massive API bills caused by cache misses.
