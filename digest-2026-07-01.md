## 💡 Coding Tip of the Day
**Use Prompt Caching Safely!**
When building LLM-based applications, prompt caching misses can lead to shockingly high API bills. Always implement hard cost-guardrails and circuit breakers in your production environments to freeze activity if compute spend spikes unexpectedly.
