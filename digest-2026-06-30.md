## 💡 Coding Tip of the Day
**Managing Agentic Workflows:** As agentic AI becomes standard in development, focus on refining your *system prompts and boundary conditions* rather than giving rigid step-by-step instructions. Let the agents figure out the "how" while you strictly define the "what" and the safety constraints. Always implement cost guardrails in production to prevent runaway LLM inference loops!
