## 💡 Coding Tip of the Day
**Beware of "Vibe Coding" in Critical Path Code**
While "vibe coding" (generating entire codebases with AI and testing via intuition) is fantastic for fast prototyping, it can introduce subtle, catastrophic failure modes in critical systems. For tasks involving backups, security policies, database migrations, or financial transactions, always manually review the generated code, write exhaustive unit tests, and never skip safety verification.
