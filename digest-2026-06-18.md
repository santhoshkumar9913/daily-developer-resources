## 💡 Coding Tip
**Refine Boundary Conditions for Agentic Workflows**: As "vibe coding" scales, focus on refining your *system prompts and boundary conditions* rather than step-by-step instructions. Let the AI agents figure out the "how" while you strictly define the "what" and the safety constraints. Always implement strict cost guardrails to avoid unexpected prompt caching misses.
