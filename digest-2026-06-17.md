## 💡 Coding Tip of the Day
**Focus on System Prompts for Agentic Workflows**
As agentic workflows become standard, focus on refining your *system prompts and boundary conditions* rather than step-by-step instructions. Let the agents figure out the "how" while you strictly define the "what" and the safety constraints.
